Restaurants

A comprehensive guide to the Newport restaurant scene. It is broken up into 2 parts: Hometown Classic and Gourmet All Day. Hometown Classic are more of your dives and family fun restaurants. They are only found here and on the lower side of the price range but still has delious food. Gourmet All Day is higher classic and also higher price; These are the type of place you would make a reservation and wear a sports jacket to it.

Annie's

A classic Newport staple that is very much beloved by all the locals. It is the perfect place to get a lovely breakfast all day as well as lunch. Along with quality food, there is also quality service with friendly servers. Some popular menu items like this like Maryland Crab Eggs Benedict, the Crow’s Nest which is an English muffin with hollandaise sauce, eggs and a homemade corned beef hash, a homemade lobster salad roll, or a fried fisherman’s platter. No matter what you get it is a great way to start the day right.

Best of breakfast or lunch
Visit the Website to learn more: http://anniesnewport.com

Flo's Clam Shack

This un-fancy local institution that has been around since the 1930’s. It has been the go-to for many when they are craving classic–style fish and chips, Clam chowder, and Rhode Island's famed fried clams and quahog stuffies(a classic stuffed fired clam ). This branch even has a raw bar; perfect for all the seafood lovers.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: http://flosclamshacks.com/

Wharf Tavern

a well beloved family style restaurant built on the waterfront; great for events and parties. An amazing diverse menu including great choice from fresh RI Classic Stuffies, fresh lobster bisque, flat bread pizzas, burgers/sandwiches, to a variety of fresh fish.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://thewharfpubnewport.com/

Brick Alley Pub

Located down by the harbor, this pub is a hot local spot. It is a great place to sit down any sort of sports game. There is a unigue décor on with decorated with vintage neon signs, carousel animals and photographs of Red Sox throughout history. They offer prize-winning nachos, an all-you-can-eat salad bar and 25 beers on tap. They also offer a wide-ranging menu from Gary’s Portuguese Littleneck Clams, to Spicy Shrimp Pizza to Marinated Steak Tip Sandwich. Overall, Brick Alley may be able to please all the people all the time.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://thewharfpubnewport.com/

The Port Raw Bar and Grill

This restaurant was a good experience overall that many would agree. There is a good bar with great cocktail choices. A large diverse menu with good portions at a fair price; it goes from a selection of burger/sandwiches, fresh fish, soups, and deserts. In addition to all of that, there is an amazing ocean view.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.theportnewport.com/

22 Bowen’s

Wine Bar and Grill

This notable dining room is located on a wharf with a lovely view. The outdoor area allows for seaside dining with amazing views of the port area. But on the inside, the ambiance features wooden and brass design, giving an old-world nautical feel to the place. There is an expert wine list stands ready to serve your need for the perfect wine to match the large selection of steaks and fishes. The list includes over 600 different types of wine, with about 35 available by the glass. Enjoy it amongst the food like Tuna Tartare, Pork Belly & Lentils, fresh grilled Atlantic Salmon, and Duck Cassoulet.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.22bowens.com//

The Black Pearl

This restaurant is very good and has differing style from informal tavern, The Classy Commodore’s room, or waterfront patio. This place complete with bar, pub-style eats and amazing views of the harbor. The Black Pearl is very well known for clam chowder, giving them a big name. The formal dining room serves a menu of classic American cuisine atop linen-draped tables. There are such in there as fresh fish like Almond Crusted Salmon, Swordfish Steak, and the twin New England lobster tails. But there is still the classic tavern food like Lobster rolls, burgers, and other delightful fresh sandwiches.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.blackpearlnewport.com/

The White Horse Tavern

It is a restaurant that has cemented itself in a place in time. It is designed beautifully as a 17th-century tavern which once served as an annual meeting place for the Colonial Rhode Island General Assembly. Lunch and dinner are served here but diner requires formal wear and lunch requires anything but swimwear. Dinner menus is always new and fresh from baked escargot, Grilled Native Sword or beef Wellington.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: https://whitehorsenewport.com/

Restaurant Bouchard

it is a delightful eating establishment that is connected to an inn, but we will only be talking about the food. It has a lovely view of the ocean and classy décor along with the staff is heavily attentive. Although the food is on higher on the price range, it is work it if you got it. The chef has many original and unique dishes such as Tarte à la Tomate et au Fromage(Warm Tomato Tart with Boursin and Goat Cheese Topped with a Truffle Aioli) to Escargots fourrées à l’Ail au Persil façon “Gratinée” (Escargots flavored with Parsley and Garlic, Topped with a Mousseline Sauce then Browned) to Homard et St. Jacques Cardinal Gratinés(Re-stuffed Roasted Lobster and Scallops with Truffle, Gruyere Cheese and Lobster Sauce). But Most importantly, at the start of meal, order the crème brulee because it is worth it.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: http://bouchardnewport.com/the-restaurant/

Jo’s American bistro

One of the best dinning experiences in the area ant a very fair price. There is an amazing ambiance and great attentive wait staff. The menu also features some great twist on American classics to get a more refined taste. Meals as such include duck and lobster quesadillas, Bacon onion jam burgers, and a duck breast with a ginger glaze, grilled apples, and butternut squash.

Best of Lunch or dinner
Visit the Website to learn more: http://josamericanbistro.com/

Bouchard Inn & Restaurant

This charming inn connected to a lovely restaurant. It has bright, homey rooms with private bathrooms, air-conditioning offer sitting areas, flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi, as well as minifridges and microwaves. Upgraded rooms add kitchenettes. It is a short walk from the International Tennis Hall of Fame and short drive from Fort Adams State Park. Continental breakfast each morning and complimentary parking included.

Average 99$ a night
Visit the Website to learn more: http://bouchardnewport.com

Hotel Viking

Dating from the 1920s, this upscale landmark hotel is a short walk from Long Wharf Mall Shopping Center and the International Tennis Hall of Fame & Museum. The elegant rooms features period furnishings, and elegant fabrics, along with modern conviences with flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. there also suites with separate living areas with pull-out sofas, minifridges and fireplaces. There's a polished restaurant serving American fare with rooftop bar and afternoon tea service. Other amenities at the hotel are a spa and salon, a fitness center with an indoor pool, event space and a shuttle to downtown Newport.

Average 99$ a night
Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.hotelviking.com/

Mill Street Inn

A stylish all-suite hotel set in an old 1850s sawmill with short walk to the Block Island ferry landing, and the International Tennis Hall of Fame & Museum. There are plush, contemporary suites, many with exposed-brick walls. It had featured high-thread-count sheets, goose-down feather beds, designer toiletries, fridges, and living areas. There are also the modern conviences with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Some add separate bedrooms, private balconies, an additional level or pull-out sofas. Breakfast is complimentary. It is served on a seasonal rooftop deck as is the complimentary afternoon tea.

Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.millstreetinn.com/

The Newport Harbor Hotel and Marina

Surrounded by the lovely streets and waters of Newport, this hotel overlooks Newport Harbor and is a short walk from the Long Wharf Mall Shopping Center and the Block Island ferry. The casual rooms with a maritime theme and are equiped with all the perks; This includes coffeemakers, whirlpool tubs, flat-screen TVs, Blu-Ray players, and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms have extras like balconies and harbor views. Other amenities include a seafood restaurant, a marina for boats, a heated indoor saltwater pool, a sundeck, saunas and laundry facilities.

Average 85$ a night
Visit the Website to learn more: https://www.newporthotel.com/
